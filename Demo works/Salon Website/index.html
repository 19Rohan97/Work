    About

    We Are JAJI's Salon & Spa

    Jaji's Salon & Spa is an ultra-modern Salon and Spa concept that places customer experience above everything. Our level of service is as amazing as our interiors and is designed to make you feel special, beautiful and rejuvenated as you leave our premises

    With 2 floors of treatment space, it is family friendly and men, women and children are welcome. Our staff is the best bar none and we take pride in keeping it that way

    We also take special care with your health and hygiene. Our instruments are UV-sterilized and we drape new sheets for every use

    So, come and visit Jaji's Salon & Spa in Trivandrum today and discover a dramatic you!
